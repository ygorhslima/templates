Last Christmas is a graceful and refined script typeface, blending elegance with a festive charm. Its swirling, intricate strokes capture the warmth and joy of the holiday season, making it the perfect choice for enchanting designs that evoke celebration and love.

Use underscore _ anywhere in a word to create a swash. 
Example: Santa _Claus

Use multiple underscores to make different swashes. 
Example: Yule_________time
